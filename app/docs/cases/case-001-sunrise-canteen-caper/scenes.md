# Scenes - The Sunrise Canteen Caper

## Scene 1: Canteen Exterior
**Location:** Outside the school canteen  
**Time of Day:** Monday, 7:30 AM  
**Atmosphere:** Quiet morning, students haven't arrived yet

### Description
The canteen is a single-story building with large windows. A "CLOSED" sign hangs on the door. Mrs. Tan's bicycle is parked outside. You can see her inside, looking distressed.

### Interactive Elements
- Bicycle (can examine lock)
- Canteen door (locked from inside)
- Window view into cash register area

### Available Clues
- None at exterior (must enter canteen)

---

## Scene 2: Cash Register Area
**Location:** Inside canteen, main counter  
**Time of Day:** Monday, 7:45 AM  
**Atmosphere:** Clean but Mrs. Tan is visibly worried

### Description
The cash register sits on a clean counter. Behind it is the strongbox, now open. Receipt rolls are neatly arranged. A calculator sits next to the register. Everything looks orderly â€” except Mrs. Tan's worried face.

### Interactive Elements
- Cash register (examine sales data)
- Strongbox (empty, lock intact)
- Calculator (has fingerprints)
- Receipt printer (check Friday's receipts)

### Available Clues
- **Clue #1:** Friday's Sales Receipt (shows lunch & recess totals)
- **Clue #2:** Monday's Cash Count Sheet (actual money remaining)
- **Clue #3:** Calculator with fingerprints

---

## Scene 3: Storage Room
**Location:** Back room behind counter  
**Time of Day:** Monday, 8:00 AM  
**Atmosphere:** Dim lighting, shelves with supplies

### Description
A small storage room with cleaning supplies, extra chairs, and food inventory. There's a back door that leads to the school courtyard. A security log clipboard hangs near the door.

### Interactive Elements
- Back door (check if forced open)
- Security log (sign-in sheet for after-hours access)
- Shelves (search for hidden items)

### Available Clues
- **Clue #4:** Security Access Log (who entered after school)
- **Clue #5:** Backpack found behind shelf (contains $50)

---

## Scene 4: Interview Room
**Location:** Principal's office  
**Time of Day:** Monday, 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM  
**Atmosphere:** Formal, tense

### Description
You interview each suspect one by one. Each gives their alibi for Friday evening. Your job is to spot inconsistencies and match their stories to the evidence.

### Suspects Present
1. Mr. Lim (Cleaner)
2. Miss Chen (Science Teacher)
3. Alex (Student Helper)

### Available Clues
- **Clue #6:** Interview transcripts with timestamps
- **Clue #7:** Alibi verification checklist

---

## Progression Notes

Students must:
1. Start at **Canteen Exterior** (introduces the crime)
2. Move to **Cash Register Area** (collect financial evidence)
3. Investigate **Storage Room** (find access log and hidden backpack)
4. Conduct **Interview Room** questioning (match alibis to evidence)
5. Solve math puzzles to calculate the discrepancy
6. Use logical deduction to identify the culprit
