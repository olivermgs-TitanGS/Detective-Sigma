- Cap daily usage (prevent 5-hour grinding sessions)

Use addiction psychology for **good** (learning Math) not **harm** (gambling/exploitation). Every reward requires mastering PSLE syllabus.

Our answer: **Yes, if done transparently.** Better kids are addicted to Math practice than TikTok/YouTube. Parents have full control to disable any feature.
